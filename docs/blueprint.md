# **App Name**: City Planner Crew UI

## Core Features:

- About Us Page: Introduce the City Planner Crew and the purpose of the application, including descriptions of the 4 AI agents.
- Bot UI: Present an interface with descriptions for each bot: Raw Material Supplier, Finance Manager, Environment Check, and Builder.
- City Layout Input: Provide input fields and checkboxes on a page to describe the desired city layout and features.
- Raw Material Analysis: AI tool to analyze the city plan and generate a list of all necessary raw materials with estimated quantities and categorization.
- Cost Estimation & Optimization: AI tool to convert the material list into a cost estimate and automatically optimize the plan to fit within a budget of â‚¹10,00,000.
- Environmental Impact Assessment: AI tool to evaluate the environmental impact of the city plan and suggest eco-friendly alternatives, also calculating a green score for the design.
- City Blueprint Generation: AI tool to generate a final city layout blueprint based on the user inputs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#89B4FA), reflecting reliability and forward-thinking planning. It is bright enough to contrast with a dark background, but not so bold that it feels 'loud'.
- Background color: Dark blue (#29335C), approximately the same hue as the primary color but heavily desaturated and darkened, establishing a modern, serious, but not overwhelming or threatening, tone.
- Accent color: Teal (#469990), which will provide contrast for calls to action and status indicators.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines to bring a computerized, techy feel, and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text to maintain a modern, machined look.
- Use clean, geometric icons to represent each of the city infrastructure and material types, maintaining a balance between aesthetic appeal and ease of recognition.
- A clean, grid-based layout is required to showcase city information.
- Subtle, animated transitions to reflect dynamic simulations and data updates.
